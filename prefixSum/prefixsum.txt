Use prefix sum when you need to find the sum of numbers between two positions in a list many times, or when you need to find subarrays whose sum equals a given number.

It helps you avoid adding the same numbers again and again.

Now to find sum from index 1 to 3:
sum(1,3) = prefix[3] - prefix[0] = 10 - 1 = 9
